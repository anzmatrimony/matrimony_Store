2017 Girl’s League Gala Day

Dear Parents and Guardians,

Your child has shown interest in representing Barnier at the Girl’s rugby league gala day. As we have had more interest than spots available, we will be holding a training sessions/trails on Thursday (30/3) morning at 8am. All children will know if they are successful in making the team at this session. For all successful girls, will be a second training session next Monday (3/4) morning at 8am.

When- Wednesday 5th April 2017
where- Whalan Reserev, Ellsworth Drive, Whalan
Cost- nil
Uniform- Students will be given a rugby league jersey to wear on the day. The girls can either wear shorts (no pockets) or tights. Football boots or runners can be worn. Mouthguards are encouraged, but not compulsory.
Transport- Parents are responsible for student transportation. If you are able to transport your child, please indicate on the permission slip below. We will confirm transportation arrangements on Monday 3rd April.

If you can take children other than your own, the school will require to sight and photocopy the following:

  • Drivers licence
  • The registration papers of the vehicle in which the children will be travelling.

If you don’t already have one, we ask that you also complete a “working with children check”. This is free of charge and can be done by going to https://wwwccheck.ccyp.nsw.gov.au/Applictions/Application# . Please follow the prompts as a volunteer and bring your verification to the office by Friday 31st March. We greatly appreciate this for the safety of the children.

Please return your note to the office by Friday 31st March 2017.
