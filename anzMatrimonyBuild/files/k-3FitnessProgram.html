K-3 Fitness Program

Dear parents and Caregivers,

The Fitness Program will continue in 2017 starting Term 2. Students from year 3 are invited to participate to allow for an increased number of students to be involved. The program will be run every Monday morning between 8:15-8:35am starting 1st May with students required to meet under the COLA. The junior Fitness Program provides students the opportunity to incorporate more exercise into their weekly routines by participating in fun games and challenges. It is also allows for students to make new friends and socialise with students from other grades.

Unfortunately, the program can only accommodate 25 students per term. If your child is unsuccessful in the first round, they will be kept on waiting list for the following terms.

If you are interested in your child participating in the program, please fill in the permission slip and medical information below. If you have any further questions, please do not hesitate to contact Miss Kunovsky
