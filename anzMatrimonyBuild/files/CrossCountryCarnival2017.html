Cross Country Carnival 2017 - Information Note

Dear parents,

The Barnier Cross country Carnival is a K – 6 carnival. All students are expected to attend the carnival as there will be races in which all students can participate (although only students turning 8 this year or older will be eligible to qualify for the Blacktown Zone Carnival). There will be no supernision at school.

When Tuesday 28th March, 2017 (Term 1, Week 10)
Where Paterson Reserve, Quakers Hill
Clothing School hat is compulsory. Students are encouraged to wear sunscreen, house colours and sensible running shoes.
Food/Drinks: There is no canteen at the oval. Children will need to take their recess (3-6 only), and plenty of water to drink.
SchoolCanteen All students will be back in time for lunch and are able to order their lunch in the morning. Students also have the opportunity to purchase a Cross Country Lunch event pack, but orders must be made by Tuesday 21st March 2017 through the office. K-2 students will be back before recess and are able to use the canteen at recess.
Permission Permission has been granted in the ‘All-In-One Permission Note’, completed at the beginning of 2017, for your child to walk to Paterson Reserve for school events. If you are aware that you did not sign this section of the note and you would like your child to attend the carnival, please contact your class teacher or download a permission note from the school website. Where a permission note is required, please return to your child’s class teacher by Tuesday 21st March 2017.
