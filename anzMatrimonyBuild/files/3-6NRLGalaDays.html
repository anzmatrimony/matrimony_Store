22nd March 2017

3-6 NRL Gala Days

Parents /Caregivers

Bariner is excited to announce that we will be participating in two NRL Gala days. The Gala days are open to open to all students in years 3-6 regardless of ability or experience. Students will participate in a number of games on the day against schools from around the Blacktown and Mount Druitt districts.

The day is being run and coordinated by the NRL Schools Program. They will be on hand to referee each game and ensure that students learn to play the game in safe respectful manner.

Primary Day

The first is all girls competition and Barnier will be putting through two teams. One for girls in Years 3 & 4 and one for girls in Year 5 & 6.

Panthers Cup

The second is the Panthers cup and Barnier will be putting through two teams. A junior Boys (9s and 10s) and a Senior Boys (11s – 12s) team.

Dates and Venues

  • Girls Primary Day – Wednesday 5th of April at Whalan Reserve
  • Panthers Cup – Tuesday 16th of May at Whalan Reserve

Transport and Cost

  • There is no cost attend the Gala day, however, students must be able to make their way to and from the venue.

As this stage we are unsure of numbers for each team. If there is a large response, trails will be held. If there is not, a team will be chosen from the returned notes.

Expression of interest notes are due to Mr Bowen by Friday the 25th of April. If your child is selected, they will be given another note regarding transport to and from the venue. We would love to see as many parents to cheer on our teams.
