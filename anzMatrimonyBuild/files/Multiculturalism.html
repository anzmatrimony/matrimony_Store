Public Speaking Activity – Multiculturalism

Term 1

During Term 1, your child’s teacher will be assessing your child’s public speaking skills. This includes presentation skills, expression, eye contact, engaging the audience, presentation of content, volume and pitch of voice.

To successfully achieve these skills, your child is required to discuss one of the topics from the below list relating to multiculturalism. They will need to prepare a speech at home, which needs to show an understanding of multiculturalism and be well developed and interesting. Students should include a balance of personal opinion and information in their speeches.

The presentation needs to be 2-3 minutes in length and palm cards can be used for prompting.

Your child must prepare for this activity for homework ready to present in Week 9 and 10 as advised by their class teacher.

Years 3 & 4 Topics

  • The same but different
  • Every family has a story
  • Kids can make a difference
  • “Boo” to racism in sport
  • Refugees
  • Racism – it stops with me
  • The Australian Flag
  • Multiculturalism in the media
  • What makes a community?
  • Harmony Day

Enjoy!
