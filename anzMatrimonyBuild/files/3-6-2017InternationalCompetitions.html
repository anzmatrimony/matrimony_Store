3-6 2017 International Competitions

Dear Parent and Carers,

Your child is invited to take part and thank you have already registered in the 2017 International competitions and Assessments for schools conducted by the University of New South Wales Educational Testing Centre. The competitions take each year in schools throughout Australia. They provide an opportunity for all students to gain a measure of their own achievement in an external situation. Two (2) new tests have been added this year.

Four (4) tests are now open to Year 2.

Please note that the sitting of these tests may not be appropriate for every student. The competitions are challenging and are not in line with the NSW Curriculum (ie. They are completely independent of the NSW Department of Education). Therefore, the school does not consider these competitions for assessment purpose as they are not measurable against any NSW assessment or curriculum. Below is a list the competition dates and the cost for each test.

Competition Subjects Competitions Sitting Dates Cost Per Entry
Digital Technologies Tuesday 23rd May $9.90

English Tuesday 1st August $9.90

To avoid disruption to regular classes, the competitions will be conducted after school on the dates above. Students will be given a short break after school and will be finished at 4pm. Please arrange for your child to be picked up by a parent/carer from the COLA at 4pm. Students will not be permitted to leave alone or with anyone other than their parent/ carer unless written permission is given by a parent/carer. If your child attends after school care on these day, you will need to notify them that your child will be arriving late that afternoon.

If you wish for your child to sit for any these tests, you will need to complete the form below and return it to their classroom teacher or office with money, for all the tests you wish your child to participate in. Total payment will only be accepted before Tuesday 4th April. No money will be accepted after this date as test papers will be ordered on this day.
