It’s that time again!! Bariner’s Annual Easter Hat Parade is on Tuesday 4th April – week 11. All family and friends are welcome to attend. This year it will be held at 9am in the COLA. Students will wear full school uniform on this day. Parents will be welcome to stay for a picnic recess with their children at the end of the parade. You may like to bring picnic rugs and chairs.

EASTER HAT MAKING

It is tradition at Barnier Public School that students make their own Easter Hats at school with their buddies before our Easter Hat Parade. It would be greatly appreciated if you could start to collect materials that would be useful to making labelled plastic bag. Teachers will let students know they will be making Easter hats with their buddy class.

Some ideas include:

  • Cardboard
  • Glitter
  • Stickers
  • Feathers
  • Eggs
  • Streamers/ribbons
  • Chicken figurines
  • Craft glue
  • Any other crafty decorations that may be suitable

If you have any questions please see your child’s teacher.
