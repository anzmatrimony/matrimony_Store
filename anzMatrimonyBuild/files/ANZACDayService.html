27th March 2017

ANZAC Day Service

Dear Parents and Guardians,

You are invited to attend the Barnier Public School ANZAC Day Service to honour and remember the courageous sacrifice of those who served our country in war and conflict.

During the service, class representatives will place flowers under the flags as symbol of remembrance.

All students are encouraged to bring in flowers on the day to place at the Lone Pine tree outside E Block as a sign of respect.

When: Wednesday, 26th
where: Barnier Public School COLA
Time: 10:00 am
