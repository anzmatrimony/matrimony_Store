Dear parents and Caregivers,

As mentioned in the previous commitment note, Blacktown Music of Performing Arts Festival (BMPAF) is grand performance at wyndham College in term 3, 2017, where parents and friends can come along to hear the vast musical talents of our students.


As the Barnier Gleeks Choir performs in unison with students from other schools, it is a requirement that your child attends the three rehearsals at Lynwood Park Public School. The first rehearsal is on Thursday 16th March, 2016, followed by the 2nd rehearsal on the 31st may and the 3rd rehearsal on the 9th August from 9:00am-12pm. At the rehearsals the conductors will ensure that your child is well prepared for the festival.


To ease the cost for families, we organised to share a bus with Bert Oldfield PS to transport our students from Barnier Public school to Lynwood Park Public School for the rehearsal dates. The total cost of the bus for each student, for the three rehearsals is $25.


Please fill in the permission slip below and return to Mrs Green before Thursday.


The last day for payment of this event will be Friday 29th March, 2016. Further information will be sent regarding performance day closer to the date.


Regards,

Danielle Green
