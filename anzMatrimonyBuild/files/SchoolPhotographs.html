School Photographs

Monday 27th March, 2017

Dear parents/Caregivers,

School photographs will be taken at school on Monday 27th March, 2017. Students are required to wear full school summer uniform.

Envelope for individual student photographs accompany this note. Please complete the order from on the envelope and return it to your child’s class teacher on Monday 27th March, 2017. Alternatively, online ordering is also available this year at www.advancedlife.com.au. Please see enclosed flyer for online ordering information.

Should you wish to have a family photograph taken, please collect a photo envelope from the school office foyer. These photos are for school aged students attending the school and no family photo will be taken unless an order from has been given to the photographers on Monday 27th March, 2017 beginning from 8:15 am in the school hall.

Please read carefully the instructions regarding payment, noting that all laye orders placed with the photographer may incur a late/search fee.

All late orders will need to be ordered through advanced Life directly, using the details provided on your order envelope. NO LATE ORDERS WILL BE ACCEPTED THROUGH THE SCHOOL.

Payments can be made by:

Cash – place the correct money in your envelopes as the photographers do not carry change.
Money Order money orders must be made payable to Advanced Life.
Credit Card Payments – Credit card payments will only be accepted for online orders.

Multiple orders;
Parents can place the multiple orders for children by either placing all the orders in one envelope or handing in multiple order envelopes clearly labelled e.g. ‘Mum’s Order’ or ‘Dad’s Order’
