AMENDED NOTE

2017 Girl’s League Gala Day

Dear Parent and Carers,

Your child is invited to take part and thank you have already registered in the 2017 International competitions and Assessments for schools conducted by the University of New South Wales Educational Testing Centre. The competitions take each year in schools throughout Australia. They provide an opportunity for all students to gain a measure of their own achievement in an external situation. Two (2) new tests have been added this year.

Four (4) tests are now open to Year 2.

Please note that the sitting of these tests may not be appropriate for every student. The competitions are challenging and are not in line with the NSW Curriculum (ie. They are completely independent of the NSW Department of Education). Therefore, the school does not consider these competitions for assessment purpose as they are not measurable against any NSW assessment or curriculum. Below is a list the competition dates and the cost for each test.

To avoid disruption to regullar classes and maximising the potential tor student success, the competitions will be conducted BEFORE school on the dates above. The school is moving to a morning testing timeslot. Tests will commence at 730am with a conclusion at 8:30

If you have not already registered for your child to sit for any of these tests, you will need to complete the form below and return it to their classroom teacher or office with money, for all the tests you wish your child to participate in. Total payment will only be accepted before Tuesday 4th April. No money will be accepted after this date as test papers will be ordered on this day. If you are already registered the only change is the 7:3oam start time and the inclusion of 2 more tests.
