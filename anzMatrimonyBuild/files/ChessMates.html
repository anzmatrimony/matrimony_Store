Chess Mates

Barnier Public School is introducing an extra- curricular chess club called ‘Chess Mates’. Chess Mates will begin in Term 2 and will run during lunch times once a week. Students in Years 2-6 who are interested in chess and looking to improve their chess skills are encouraged to join. Student skill level and experience will range from beginning to advanced.

Chess Mates aim is to provide an opportunity to learn and;

  • Utilise chess skills
  • Improve critical thinking and concentration
  • Develop decision-making and planning skills
  • Learn good sportsmanship – the ability to win and lose with grace
  • Make new friends and have fun!

Chess Mates has a 64 student limit and priority will be given to those students who return their notes first to chess teacher Mr Bowen and Mr Whitney. Those students who do not make the first 64 students will be held on a waiting list. All students in ‘Chess Mates’ will be required to attend chess session at lunch. Failure to attend chess session may result in that child losing their spot to another student.
